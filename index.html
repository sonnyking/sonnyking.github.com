A bit about myself

I’m a passionate technologist with an eye for design that brings 16 years experience crafting high fidelity user experiences to my role of Principal Design Technologist at frog. A UI/UX specialist with a broad yet deep skill set I’ve contributed to the successful delivery of a large number of innovation programs as well as consumer facing properties for clients such as AT&T, Chase, Disney, GE, Intel, Mozilla and Telstra among others.

My technical background is diverse and modern leveraging advanced UI/UX technologies such as HTML5, JavaScript, WebGL, ActionScript, Lua tied to Node.js, Ruby and Java backends. This expertise translates well to other smaller form factor embedded platforms/systems with web/markup UX stacks sitting atop low-level C cores. While not normally considered a Software Architect or Engineer per se I’m very comfortable contributing to delivery focused programs in similar capacities leveraging test/behavior driven development, Agile processes and modern best practices.

Prior to joining frog I was the Director of Multimedia Technology in the interactive practice at 22Squared, a large independent advertising agency servicing Fortune 500 clients across the country. An enthusiastic design technology evangelist I’ve enjoyed opportunities to share my passion with others, as Adjunct Professor of Web Development at Southern Polytechnic University and by assisting enterprises and individuals to broaden their exposure to progressive, forward thinking practices whenever possible.
